Honey Garlic Chicken Noodles

Authentic Honey Garlic Chicken Noddles

This chicken noodle recipe is a comfort dish that can be eaten on rainy days. The chicken thighs cooked with honey and garlic creates a savory and tangy texture creating the perfect combination when eaten with noddles. It will leave you and your guests feeling full and satified.

Ingredients

This recipe yields 6 servings

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup lower-sodium soy sauce
  • 6 medium garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce or soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Siracha chile sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons tap water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 16 ounces fresh lo mein noodles, cooked according to package directions
  • 3 large (1/2-ounce each) scallions, thinly sliced, plus more for garnish
  • white sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix in the honey, soy sauce, garlic, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, Sriracha, Shaoxing wine, ginger, sesame oil, and salt in a 6-quart slow cooker. Place the chicken thighs in a flat layer into the honey mixture.
  3. Cover and cook until chicken is tender, about 3 hours on high and 5 hours on low.
  4. Combine the water and starch on a asmall bowl until smooth. Place the cooked chicken onto a cutting board and shred with a fork.
  5. On high heat, pour in the cornstarch mixture and stir until slightly thickened, for about 30 minutes. Turn off slow cooker. Put the shredded chicken back into the slow cooker along with cooked noodles and scallions; toss to coat.
  6. Divide the mixture evenly among 6 bowls, and garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.
