Lasagna Recipe

Homemade Lasagna

This Lasagna recipe might be time consuming, but the end result is mind-blowing. Below you will find detailed step-by-step intructions that will guide you through the entire process of making this Lasagna recipe.If you chose to replace or omit some of the ingredients go ahead! but it might not taste the same as the original version.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of tomato sauce
  • Salt, black pepper
  • Fennel seeds, italian seasoning, fresh parsley, dried basil leaves
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta
  • 1 Egg

Directions

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna cool before serving

Layering the Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then add the rest of the parmesan cheese on top

How to cook lasagna

The lasagna will take approximately 50 minutes to cook ina an oven pre heated to 375 degrees F.Cover with foil for the first 25 minutes, then cook for another 25 minutes uncovered.Then take it out of the oven and let it rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before serving.
