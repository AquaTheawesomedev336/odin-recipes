Chinese Styled Pork Congee

Pork Congee

This chinese version of pork congee is simple and easy tomake perfect for lazy people who are craving a comfort and filling meal. The whole process wont take more than 2 hours, the cooking time takes the longest, so just sit and relax while you wait for your congee to be ready.

Ingredients

This recipe yields 4 servings

  • 2 1/2 cups of water or chicken broth
  • 1 cup of jasmine rice
  • 1 carrot
  • 8 dried shitake mushrooms
  • 20 grams of tofu skin
  • 20 grams of lean pork
  • 1 century egg
  • scallion
  • ginger
  • salt
  • white pepper
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Directions

  1. Wash the rice, and add it into the boiling water
  2. Add the soaked tofu skin and mushroom into the pot.
  3. Stir a few times then let it cook for 30 minutes on low heat.
  4. After the 30 minutes, stir it with a whisk, add in the chopped carrot, meat and egg, then let it cook for another 25 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce and garnish it with spring onions, them it's ready to be served.
